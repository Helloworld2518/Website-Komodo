Full Day Komodo With Speedboat

1 hari di Pulau Komodo dengan kapal speedboat. Menjelajahi destinasi terkenal seperti Pandar, Pink Beach, Komodo. Paket sudah termasuk hotel dan konsumsi serta transportasi

Rundown and information

Time Destination/Activity About the destination
05.45 - 06.00 Hotel In the morning at 6 am, we will pick you up at the hotel where you are staying. After that we will board the speedboat that we have prepared.
First Destination
07.30 - 08.30 Padar Padar is one of the main destinations in Labuan Bajo. Here we will trek up the stairs and enjoy the very beautiful views. There are several posts here, so those with less stamina can reach the top, no problem. Still get a cool view.
Second Destination
09.00 - 10.00 PINK BEACH Pink Beach is a destination favored by beach lovers with a different atmosphere because of its unique color. Pink. Here we can beach or swim in clear water.
Third Destination
11.00 - 12.00 KOMODO DRAGON We are heading to one of the 7 Wonders of the World, namely Komodo Island. Here there are 3 route choices, namely: short track, medium track and long track. This choice depends on our wishes. However, we only recommend a short trek which takes approximately 30 minutes to walk. Because it's quite hot here, guys.
Fourth Destination
12.30 - 13.30 MANTA POINT Manta Point is a paradise for those of you who like snorkeling because here you can feel the sensation of swimming with Manta Fish or Manta Rays. Relax, a professional Tour Guide will accompany you.
Fifth Destination
14.00 - 15.00 TAKA MAKASSAR Taka Makassar or often called Gusung Island is a curved form of sand. The water here is very clear. You can play in the water or relax.
Sixth Destination
16.30 - 17.00 KANAWA The time has come on Kanawa Island, you can do snorkeling with thousands of fish. If you are not interested in snorkeling, here you can do relaxing activities on an inhabited island managed by Kanawa Resort..
Homecoming
17.00 - 17.30 HOTEL We will arrive at Labuan Bajo Harbor. Then you will be escorted by our transport team to return to the hotel to rest. See you
