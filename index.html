OPEN TRIP 3 DAY 2 NIGH
Day Activity Explain
DAY 1 TRANSFER IN – HALFDAY SAILING KOMODO TOUR (L&D) Welcome to the Meeting Point (Hotel/Airport in Labuan Bajo) at 09.00 WITA. You will be picked up by our professional team to be taken to Labuan Bajo Harbor, then you are invited to board the boat that we have prepared. We will introduce you to all the crew members who will serve you, such as the Ship Captain, Master and Chef & Guide. The first day you will visit a total of 3 main destinations. Sailing starts by visiting Kelor Island which is beautiful for snorkeling, and climbing the photo hill at the top of the hill on Kelor Island is one of the best photo spots. After that, the next destination is Manjarite Island, which is famous as one of the best coral reef snorkeling spots. Until finished, we will head to Kalong Island to await the arrival of Bats and Sunset. After the guests have dinner, the next free program is and we lean back and rest here. Stay on the Ship.
DAY 2 FULLDAY SAILING KOMODO TOUR (B,L & D) On the second day, the number of tourist destinations we will visit is 5 destinations. In the morning we leave to start our journey by heading to Padar Island which requires quite a bit of trekking. Satisfied in Padar, we will head to Long Beach (Pink Beach which is wider) to play on the beach, snorkel and take video documentation. Until finished, then our boat will continue to Komodo Island. During activities on Komodo Island, participants will be accompanied by a Komodo ranger. , after leaving Komodo Island, the Pinisi ship continues its journey to Manta Point to swim or snorkel with Manta Ray. Then you will head to Taka Makassar to take photos in the very clear sea and continue our journey to Kanawa Island, arriving at Kanawa Island, trip participants have dinner and rest.
DAY 3 HALFDAY SAILING KOMODO TOUR– TRANSFER OUT (L,D) Morning call to see the sunrise on Kanawa Island, while on Kanawa Island guests can enjoy the best snorkeling experience seeing beautiful coral reefs & swimming surrounded by hundreds of nemo fish. At 12.00 the Pinisi ship arrives at KP 3 Harbor, Labuan Bajo City, you will be taken back to Labuan Bajo Harbor, there you will be picked up by land transportation and taken to the Drop Point (Labuan Bajo Hotel/Airport). The tour ended here on a memorable note with us

Destination Activity
HOTEL You will be picked up by our professional team at the hotel where you are staying.
MIRROR STONE GOA Mirror rock cave is a cave or tunnel located in a dark rocky hill. The area of ​​this cave is 19 hectares and the height is around 75 meters. Sunlight enters the cave through the cave walls and reflects its light on the stone walls so that it reflects small light into other areas in the cave
RANGKO GOA To go to Rangko Cave, we will board the boat that we have prepared. Here we can do swimming activities and also just relax on the beaches around Goa.

Labuan Bajo Tour 1 Day (fullday Open Deck)


In the morning at 6 am, we will meet at Meeting Point (Labuan Bajo Harbor) to prepare and board the ship that we have prepared. Your trip will start by visiting Padar Island. After that we will move to Pink Beach. Until you are satisfied, you will be invited to continue your journey to one of the 7 Wonders of the World, namely Komodo Island.


Once you are satisfied walking around seeing and taking photos with Komodo accompanied by a Ranger (Komodo Tour Guide), you will sail the ocean again to visit Manta Point. Here you can do snorkeling activities with Manta Ray. After that, you will be taken back to the Drop Point (Labuan Bajo Harbor). The Explore Komodo Islands Tour ended impressively with us :).
